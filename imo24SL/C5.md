*** Problem Statement ***

Let $N$ be a positive integer. Geoff and Ceri play a game starting with $\{1, 2, \dots, N\}$. In each move, a player chooses $(k, n)$ with $k \geq 0$ and $n$ on the board, then erases every $s$ such that $2^k \mid n - s$. The last person to erase a number **loses**. Geoff goes first. Determine all $N$ such that Geoff can force a win.
