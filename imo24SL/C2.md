*** Problem Statement ***

Let $n$ be a positive integer. Fill the integers $1$ to $n^2$ into an $n \times n$ board, one per cell. For every $d \mid n$ with $1 < d < n$, partition the board into non-overlapping $d \times d$ sub-boards. We say $n$ is a **cool number** if, for every such $d$, the sum in each $d \times d$ sub-board is **not** divisible by $d$. Determine all even cool numbers.
