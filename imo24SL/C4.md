*** Problem Statement ***

On a $2024 \times 2023$ board, a snail starts at any cell in the top row and wants to reach any cell in the bottom row, moving step-by-step to adjacent cells. There are 2022 hidden monsters, one in each intermediate row, no two in the same column. If the snail hits a monster, it restarts from the top. Determine the minimum number of attempts needed to guarantee success regardless of monster locations.
