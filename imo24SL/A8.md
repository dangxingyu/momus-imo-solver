*** Problem Statement ***

Let $p \ne q$ be coprime positive integers. Determine all infinite sequences $a_1, a_2, \dots$ of positive integers such that for all $n \geq 1$,
$$
\max(a_n, a_{n+1}, \dots, a_{n+p-1}) - \min(a_n, a_{n+1}, \dots, a_{n+p-1}) = p,
$$
and
$$
\max(a_n, a_{n+1}, \dots, a_{n+q-1}) - \min(a_n, a_{n+1}, \dots, a_{n+q-1}) = q.
$$
