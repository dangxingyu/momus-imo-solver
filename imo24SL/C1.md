*** Problem Statement ***

Let $n$ be a positive integer. A class of $n$ students run $n$ races, each with a strict ranking. A student is eligible for a rating $(a, b)$ if they place in the top $b$ in at least $a$ of the races. Their final score is the maximum value of $a - b$ across all such ratings. Find the maximum possible sum of all $n$ students' final scores.
